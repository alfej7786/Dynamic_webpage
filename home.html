    • Welcome to SMC!

    SMC's mission is to create a safer online environment for teenagers by providing them with the necessary knowledge, tools, and
    resources to make informed decisions while using social media platforms. SMC offers a range of services aimed at promoting
    online safety among teenagers.These services may include educational workshops, online resources, interactive tools, and
    support networks tailored to the needs of teenagers.
